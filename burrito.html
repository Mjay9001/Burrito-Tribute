The Burrito

bur·ri·to

A burrito is a Mexican dish, consisting of a tortilla rolled around a filling, typically of beans or ground or shredded beef.

This page is a tribute to the humble burrito. For more info, visit the Burrito wiki in the link below.

Why do I love burritos?

Burritos do not provide companionship, they do not provide me wealth, nor do they heal my wounds. What burritos provide is pure satisfaction. The combination of flavors, the elegant packaging, it truly is the perfect vessel to contain my favorite ingredients for an "on the go" Mexican banquet. Dare I say, the perfect meal?
